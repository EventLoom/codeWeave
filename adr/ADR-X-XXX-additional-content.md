# Context

The CodeWeave framework is composed of a large library of **Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)** that govern the behaviour, structure, and operational philosophy of software systems.

These decisions exist to reduce architectural ambiguity and prevent teams from repeatedly re-litigating the same design debates under time pressure.

The full framework contains **35+ decisions** covering areas including:

- structural architecture
- operational control
- security boundaries
- dependency management
- observability
- incident response
- governance control

However, this repository is intentionally published as a **public sample** of the framework.

To maintain clarity and demonstrate the philosophy of CodeWeave without publishing the entire library, only a **small subset of ADRs** are included here.

---

# Decision

This repository will include **only a limited selection of ADRs** from the CodeWeave framework.

These ADRs are sufficient to demonstrate the **design philosophy and governance model** but do not represent the complete decision library.

The full framework is distributed separately as the **CodeWeave ADR Framework Pack**.

---

# Rationale

Publishing a limited subset of decisions allows this repository to:

- demonstrate how CodeWeave constrains architectural decisions
- show how the framework improves operational clarity
- provide a realistic example of ADR-driven engineering governance

without exposing the full library of engineering doctrine.

---

# Consequences

Readers of this repository should expect that:

- some referenced decision domains are **not included in this sample**
- certain architectural constraints may appear **incomplete or intentionally limited**
- the repository represents **a demonstration of the framework rather than the entire framework**

The full CodeWeave decision library is available as part of the **CodeWeave ADR Framework Pack**.
